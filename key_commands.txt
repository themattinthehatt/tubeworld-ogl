Free Mode (SPACE to toggle to Bound Mode)
A - rotate left
D - rotate right
W - rotate up
S - rotate down
Up arrow - move forward
Down arrow - move back
Left arrow - move left
Right arrow - move right
Shift + Up arrow - move up
Shift + Down arrow - move down
E - decrease speed
R - increase speed
Q - reset to initial position

Bound Mode (SPACE to toggle to Free Mode)
PathCircle
    A - rotate left
    D - rotate right
    W - rotate up
    S - rotate down
    Up arrow - move forward
    Down arrow - move back
    Left arrow - move left
    Right arrow - move right
    Shift + Up arrow - move up
    Shift + Down arrow - move down
    E - decrease speed
    R - increase speed
    Q - reset to initial position

PathRandom
    A - decrease horizontal angle curvature
    D - increase horizontal angle curvature
    W - increase vertical angle curvature
    S - decrease vertical angle curvature
    Up arrow - increase speed linearly
    Down arrow - decrease speed linearly
    Shift + Up arrow - increase speed multiplicatively
    Shift + Down arrow - decrease speed multiplicatively

PathUser
    A - turn tube left
    D - turn tube right
    W - turn tube up
    S - turn tube down
    Up arrow - move forward in tube
    Down arrow - move backward in tube
    Left arrow - move tube left
    Right arrow - move tube right
    Shift + Up arrow - move tube upwards
    Shift + Down arrow - move tube downwards
    E - decrease speed
    R - increase speed
    Q - reset to initial position

General
Tab - toggle between render modes
Caps - toggle between skybox modes
